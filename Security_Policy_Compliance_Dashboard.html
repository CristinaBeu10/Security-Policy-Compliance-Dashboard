About this dashboard: This tool was developed as part of the dissertation "Securing Hybrid Cloud Environments: A Comparative Study of Multi-Cloud Security Policies" (Cristina Beu, 2026). It visualises the compliance posture of AWS, Azure, and GCP across five security domains and five regulatory frameworks. Scores are derived from documentary analysis of official provider documentation and mapped against NIST CSF 2.0, ISO/IEC 27001:2022, CSA CCM v4.0, GDPR, and NIS2.
